About-Us

The Grim Reality

In India, about 17% of the population in underweight. Whilst most of it due to the inaccesablity to healthy and nutritious food, a percentage of it is due to Anorexia.

Our Goal

Our Goal at Nutrition Based Advocates is to provide fresh, hygenic and nutritios produce to both urban and rural areas in Idia at an affordable rate as to overcome this highly overlook problem.

This Company has been started by Vihaan Mittal , Kavish Aggrawal and Aadit Dhaiya to help people facing eating disorders and make India an anorexia free country.
